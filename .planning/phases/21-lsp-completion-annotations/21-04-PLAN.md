1. `lsp21`:
   - runs the annotation script;
   - runs the reverse refs script;
   - runs `go -C dbee test ./core`;
   - runs `lsp21-locked-helpers-guard`.

   - fails if any locked helper has working-tree or staged diff;
   - greps Phase 21 reverse-FK read APIs to prove `epoch_authority.read_with_freshness` is used;
